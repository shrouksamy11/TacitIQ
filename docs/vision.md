# Vision

## Our Vision

TacitIQ aims to become an intelligent industrial knowledge platform that preserves human expertise before it disappears.

In many manufacturing companies, experienced technicians solve complex problems using years of accumulated practical knowledge. Unfortunately, this knowledge is rarely documented and is often lost when employees retire or leave the company.

Our vision is to transform tacit human experience into searchable, reusable, and intelligent organizational knowledge.

Rather than replacing human expertise, TacitIQ empowers future engineers by learning from previous experiences, maintenance records, observations, and problem-solving journeys.

We believe that every solved problem should become knowledge that helps solve the next one.
