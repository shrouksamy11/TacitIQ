# Product Principles

## 1. Knowledge First

Every solved case should become reusable organizational knowledge.

---

## 2. Human-Centered AI

AI assists engineers and technicians but never replaces their expertise.

---

## 3. Simplicity

Recording observations should take less than one minute.

---

## 4. Search Before Solve

Users should always be encouraged to search previous knowledge before creating a new case.

---

## 5. Learn Continuously

Every new case improves the organization's collective knowledge.

---

## 6. Explainable AI

AI recommendations must always include explanations and confidence levels.
