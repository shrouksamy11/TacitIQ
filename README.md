# 📖 Overview

TacitIQ is an AI-powered Industrial Knowledge Management System designed to preserve critical maintenance expertise generated by engineers and technicians inside manufacturing environments.

Instead of allowing years of operational experience to disappear when employees leave, TacitIQ captures maintenance knowledge, structures it into reusable assets, and enables organizations to retrieve previous solutions whenever similar problems occur.

The project combines Knowledge Management principles with Artificial Intelligence to support faster troubleshooting, reduce repeated failures, and improve maintenance efficiency.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Industrial organizations often rely on undocumented human expertise.

As experienced employees retire or change jobs, organizations lose valuable operational knowledge that cannot easily be recovered.

Common challenges include:

- Repeated machine failures
- Slow troubleshooting
- Poor documentation
- Knowledge silos
- Loss of maintenance expertise

---

# 💡 Solution

TacitIQ provides a centralized platform where maintenance knowledge is collected, organized, and transformed into searchable organizational intelligence.

The platform allows engineers and technicians to:

- Register maintenance cases
- Record observations
- Upload images and voice notes
- Document investigations
- Store implemented solutions
- Generate reusable knowledge assets

Future AI modules will recommend similar historical cases and assist engineers in diagnosing equipment failures.

python manage.py migrate
